This Credit Card Apporval Prediction model predict whether credit card will be approved or not. The prediction is done on the basis of various criteria like Age , Gender , Income and Employment Status etc.The dataset is taken from Uci. By using Machine Learning, the dataset is trained and test in 8:2 ratio. We use Logistic Regression Model to predict . This model gives about 79 % of accuracy ,which is good enough.
